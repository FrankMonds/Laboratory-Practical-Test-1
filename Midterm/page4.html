3rd Recommendation

"Good Will Hunting" Movie Clip

from Jonathan Jee

A clip from the popular movie, Good Will Hunting, that displays another dialogue between two characters talking about the love they have for
certain people. In the dialogue, the professor tells an evocative story about how he met his wife to the student who asked the question,
“When did you know she was the one for you?”. He builds up the story with an enticing recap of a monumental moment for the baseball community
from a specific match, exciting the student as he is told the professor got tickets for the match. However, his recap abruptly ends as the professor
says he was not there to watch the moment as he had eyes on something more important, the woman who became his wife. Through the dialogue,
the professor’s love for his late wife was emanating throughout their conversation — leaving his student with food for thought. The whole clip
exhibits love through the professor, the actors, and the writing, helping the overall film garner massive popularity despite it not being recent.
