2nd Recommendation

"A Train of Thought"

by SchyGuyy

Yet another short film that displays the intricate relationship we have with our own selves that can seep through our bond with others.
In the many beautiful shots, the main character is shown to be either jogging, laying on the grass, or walking about the city while listening to
music — all of which showing that he is zoning out. During the film, the main character can be heard talking to someone through the phone in which
he opens up to his current standstill in life, eventually helping him to finally lock his thoughts in. The film tells the story mainly through
the cinematography, underscoring the character’s current state of his thoughts, and the love of the film’s creator spewed onto his shots
and editing.
